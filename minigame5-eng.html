My name is Plato and I'm a philosopher and mathematician in ancient Greece.

I wrote many different books about my ideas and beliefs. I also founded the Academy in Athens, which became a model for modern universities.

Socrates, another great philosopher, was my teacher. I also taught another well known philosopher, Aristoteles.

My best-known work was called 'The Republic', in which I discuss the meaning of justice, the ideal state and the role of the philosopher. Some other books that I wrote include Laws, Apology, Gorgias, and Sophist.
